Sign Up for Safaricom Home Fibre

Step 1: Check Coverage

Ensure Safaricom Home Fibre is available in your area.

Visit the Safaricom website or dial *400# to check availability.

Step 2: Register for the Service

Submit your details to Safaricom.

You can register online, visit a Safaricom shop, or call customer service to initiate the signup process.

Step 3: Make Payment

Choose your preferred package and pay via M-Pesa.

Use the Safaricom PayBill number provided during registration to complete payment.

Step 4: Installation

Schedule an installation appointment.

Safaricom technicians will install the router and configure the connection at your home.
